Lessons learned:


-App cannot completely depend on 3rd party services (Facebook, Google, Amazon API's, etc) - Album of the moment
-Do not spend significant of time optimizing for performance before having regular users
-Need social interaction
-Tied to own passion, interests
-See a need to use this for the rest of your life
-Need for mobile interface
-Picture centric
-Connect users to ideas, products
-Communicate with people who share passion

Try to learn skills that will be come valuable in near future:
-mobile - Objective C, cross-platform
-new up-and-coming technologies (Node.js, Angularjs)
